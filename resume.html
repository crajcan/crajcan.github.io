Career Retrospective

What I'm trying to say in this section: What I've had to deal with: uncertainty. What I've gained dealing with that: An ability to creatively problem solve by developing workflows with short feedback loops in various problem domains. What I hope to do/gain next: Experience and expertise building reliable and modifiable systems.

I am experienced in developing in the presense of a large degree of uncertainty, working on systems that are highly resistent to being understood. At times I have even had to treat systems empirically to gain the necessary understanding required to make changes confidently. This experience has forced me to take great care in minimizing feedback loops while desigining workflows to tackle daily development tasks.
